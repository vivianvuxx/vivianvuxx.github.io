Seoul Forest

~ Seoul forest is a large park located in Seongsudong-area

~ Its famous for Cherry Blossom in the spring and Ginkgo Tree Forest in autumn

~ Seoul Forest the top 3 largest park in Korea and located in the Seongdong-gu area. The park is open since June 2005 and opended yearly round with free entrance fee. Compare to other paid cherry blossom festival that is packed with visitor and tourist, Seoul Forest is more well-known for the locals. The park content multiple facility such as horse racing track, golf course, and sport park
